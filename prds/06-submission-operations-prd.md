# PRD: Submission Operations

## 1. Purpose

Ensure the final hackathon submission is reliable, complete, and optimized for judging.

## 2. Deadline

Submissions are due EOD Tuesday, June 30, 2026.

## 3. Required Assets

- Recorded 1-3 minute demo video.
- Tweet tagging Nous Research with a short writeup.
- Discord submission-channel link.
- Typeform submission.
- Evidence bundle for internal confidence before submission.

## 4. Demo Rehearsal Requirements

- Rehearse the full live path at least three times.
- Capture one successful full run before attempting the final recording.
- Confirm no secrets or personal account details appear in the recording.
- Confirm all external service dashboards used in recording are test-mode or safe live accounts.

## 5. Backup Requirements

The user prefers fully live, but the team should still preserve evidence in case a final rehearsal fails:

- Screenshots of successful live integration events.
- Captured event logs.
- Exported memo.
- Stripe/NVIDIA/Hermes proof screenshots where safe.

These backups are evidence and rehearsal aids, not a replacement for the intended live demo.

## 6. Acceptance Criteria

- Final video is between 1 and 3 minutes.
- The first 20 seconds clearly state the problem and show the product.
- The video visibly shows a governed agentic-service trial, spend/provisioning controls, blocked action, evidence import, and final expansion decision.
- The submission text explains why Agent IC is useful, viable, and presentation-ready for enterprise buyers of agentic services.
